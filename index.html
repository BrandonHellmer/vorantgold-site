How It Works

A controlled process for requests that require more than convenience.

Every request is reviewed individually. That allows Vorant Gold to match the right sourcing path, presentation level, timing, and execution structure to the actual need instead of forcing your company into a generic ordering system.

1. Present the request

Share what needs to be handled, the outcome you want, your budget direction, and any timing, aesthetic, or operational details that matter.

2. Review the direction

Vorant Gold prepares a tailored recommendation built around the request itself — whether that involves gifting, equipment, client presentation, furnishings, sourcing, or a custom business need.

3. Approve and proceed

Once approved, Vorant Gold coordinates the proper sourcing path and execution flow with discretion, clarity, and payment-first discipline.

Service Structure

Clear economics. Private execution.

20%

Vorant Gold applies a concierge service fee to the total product or project value, plus shipping, tax, insurance, and any approved presentation or fulfillment requirements. Smaller requests are subject to a minimum service fee. Larger or more complex requests are quoted based on scope, sourcing complexity, and coordination requirements.

Some requests remain straightforward. Others require layered sourcing, elevated presentation, coordination across addresses, or custom execution. Those details are quoted according to the structure of the request itself.

Illustrative example

Project procurement: $2,000
Vorant Gold concierge fee: $400
Shipping / insurance: $75
Subtotal before tax: $2,475
Applicable sales tax: calculated as required
Final invoice total: issued prior to procurement
  • Rush timelines can be quoted case by case
  • Premium presentation can be added separately where appropriate
  • Multi-address coordination can be structured when required
