Policies

Structured to remain compliant, polished, and dependable.

These rules protect the business and keep fulfillment tight from day one.

Operating rules

  • No in-person delivery or local handoff
  • No purchases before written approval and cleared payment
  • Tracked shipping on all orders
  • Insurance and signature on higher-value shipments when needed

Alcohol policy — launch version

Alcohol may be included only as a vendor-fulfilled add-on through a properly licensed third-party retailer, winery, or gifting vendor operating in compliance with applicable law.

Vorant Gold does not directly resell, warehouse, package, or ship alcohol at launch.
