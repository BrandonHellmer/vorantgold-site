Operating standards

Structured to remain polished, dependable, and compliant.

These policies protect quality, preserve consistency, and ensure every project is handled with the discipline expected of a premium concierge service.

Fulfillment standards

  • No in-person delivery or local handoff
  • No purchasing before written approval and cleared payment
  • Tracked shipping on all orders
  • Insurance and signature on higher-value shipments where appropriate

Alcohol policy — launch version

Alcohol may be included only as a vendor-fulfilled add-on through a properly licensed third-party retailer, winery, or gifting vendor operating in compliance with applicable law.

Vorant Gold does not directly resell, warehouse, package, or ship alcohol at launch.
